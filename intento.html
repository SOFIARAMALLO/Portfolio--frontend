  • Born in Atlanta Georgia 15 de enero de 1929
  • Since he was a child, he lived the experience of a segregationist society;7 At the age of six, two white friends announced that they were not allowed to play with him.
  • King studied at Booker T. Washington High School in Atlanta. He attended neither the ninth nor the twelfth grade, and entered Morehouse College, a university reserved for black youth, at the age of 15 without having graduated from high school.
  • In 1948, he graduated in sociology (Bachelor of Arts) in the Morehouse10 and enrolled at the Crozer Theological Seminary in Chester, Pennsylvania, from where he graduated with a Bachelor of Divinity degree (licentiate in theology) on June 12, 1951. King began his studies in September of that same year. in systematic theology from Boston University, receiving the degree of Doctor of Philosophy on June 5, 1955
